Frequently Asked Questions

How does the daily quiz work?
Every day you get 5 new London trivia questions. Everyone gets the same questions each day, so you can compare scores with friends. The quiz resets at midnight.
What happens if I miss a day?
Your streak will reset to zero unless you have a Streak Freeze active. You can buy Streak Freezes in the app to protect your streak — they activate automatically when you miss a day.
What is the Bonus Round?
The Bonus Round is a one-time purchase that unlocks 5 extra questions each day, on top of your regular daily quiz. It's a great way to get more trivia practice and earn a higher daily score.
How do I restore my purchases?
Go to Settings (gear icon on the home screen) and tap "Restore purchases". This will restore any previous purchases linked to your Apple ID, including the Bonus Round unlock.
How does the leaderboard work?
Quizli uses Apple's Game Center for leaderboards. Make sure you're signed in to Game Center in your iPhone Settings. Your daily score is submitted automatically after you complete the quiz.
Can I play offline?
Yes! Quizli works fully offline. Questions are stored on your device. The app will check for new questions when you're online, but you can always play without an internet connection.
How do I change my profile or avatar?
Tap your profile icon on the home screen. From there you can change your display name, choose an avatar, and customise your profile ring colour.
Is my data private?
Absolutely. Quizli stores all data locally on your device. We don't collect any personal information, and we don't use any analytics or tracking. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
The app isn't loading or is crashing
Try closing and reopening the app. If that doesn't work, restart your iPhone. If the problem persists, make sure you're running the latest version from the App Store, and email us with details about the issue.
I have a question suggestion!
We'd love to hear it! Email us at usecommuto@gmail.com with your question idea and we'll consider adding it to the game.
