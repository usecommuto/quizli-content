Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

Data Storage

All your quiz data, streaks, and achievements are stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

No Personal Data Collection

Quizli does not require an account or login. No personal information such as your name, email, or location is ever collected.

No Analytics

We do not use any analytics services or tracking tools. Your usage of the app is completely private.

Game Center

Quizli uses Apple's Game Center for leaderboards and challenges. Your Game Center display name and scores are shared through Apple's Game Center service. This data is managed by Apple per their privacy policy.

In-App Purchases

Quizli offers optional in-app purchases processed by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Network Requests

The app periodically fetches updated quiz questions from a hosted JSON file over HTTPS. This request does not include any identifying information about you or your device.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@quizli.app.
