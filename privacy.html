Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 July 2026

Quizli is a daily London trivia game. You don't need an account to play. This policy explains what data the app uses and why.

Your Progress Stays on Your Device

Your quiz history, scores, streaks, achievements, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not upload this progress to our own servers or link it to your identity.

Advertising

The free version of Quizli shows ads provided by Google AdMob. To measure and, if you allow it, personalise these ads, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may access your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) and related device information. On first launch iOS asks for your permission via the App Tracking Transparency prompt — if you decline, you'll still see ads, but non-personalised ones. Google's handling of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Subscribing to "The Knowledge" removes all ads and this ad-related data collection.

Multiplayer (Pub Round)

When you host or join a Pub Round game, a display name you choose along with your in-game answers and scores are shared with the other players in that game. Depending on how you play, this happens over your local network (nearby devices via Bluetooth/Wi-Fi), Apple's Game Center, or our hosting provider (Supabase) for web-based games. These messages are temporary, are only used to run the game, are not tied to your identity, and are periodically deleted.

Quiz Content

The app periodically fetches updated questions and puzzles from our hosted files over HTTPS. These requests do not include personal information about you.

Game Center

Quizli uses Apple's Game Center for leaderboards, achievements, and challenges. Your Game Center display name and scores are shared through Game Center and managed by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

Quizli offers optional in-app purchases and the "The Knowledge" subscription, all processed by Apple. We never receive or store your payment details.

Children

Quizli is suitable for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ads are delivered in accordance with Google AdMob's policies.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@quizli.app.
